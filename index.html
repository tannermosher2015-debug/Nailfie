The artist

One chair.
Full attention.

Maylei is a licensed nail technician who builds nails by hand — sculpted acrylic, hard gel, and the patented Apres Gel-X system — from a single studio on Market Street.

No assembly line, no rushing you out the door. Every set is shaped, structured, and painted one-on-one, so the fit lasts and the art is yours alone. Whether it's a clean almond for everyday or hand-painted detail for the occasion, you're booking the artist, not a station number.
